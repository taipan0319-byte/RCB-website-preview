04 / Selected work

Pharmacy diligence

One unusual drug cost. A much larger story.

RCB’s examination of pharmacy-plan experience helped identify extraordinary costs and vendor issues surrounding H.P. Acthar Gel. The work illustrates what rigorous contract knowledge, claim-level diligence, and a willingness to keep asking questions can uncover.

The matter later became part of national reporting and litigation involving the drug’s manufacturer and other parties.

View the CBS 60 Minutes report ↗

PBM audits

Twenty-five plans. One hidden overcharge on every prescription.

RCB audited PBM arrangements and contract pricing performance for a regional employer health coalition made up of more than 25 Rockford-area employer plans. The audits recovered more than one million dollars owed to these employers and exposed a systemic overcharge assessed on every prescription, unknown to the employers paying it.

Once exposed, the overcharge was returned to the coalition’s members.

GLP-1 strategy

The GLP-1 surge,
met with a plan.

GLP-1 medications are reshaping pharmacy spend faster than any drug class in recent memory. RCB created and implemented a GLP-1 program that was the first of its kind: designed to reduce overall cost, reduce the cost of side effects, and improve the long-term success of the patients who take them.
